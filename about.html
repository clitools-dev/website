About CliTools.Dev

Welcome to CliTools.Dev! We are passionate about command-line interface (CLI) tools and believe in the power of a well-crafted CLI to boost developer productivity and streamline workflows.

Our mission is to create a fully automated, community-driven platformthat showcases the best CLI tools through collective wisdom. We believe in complete transparency and democratization of tool discovery - no gatekeeping, no manual approvals, just pure community consensus.
